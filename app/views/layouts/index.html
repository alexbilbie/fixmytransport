The bus stop at Knenhall is urgent need of replacing. It's been vandalised, and was partly demolished by a parked van last week. Despite a letter to the Travel West Midlands, nothing appears to be done about it.

Knenhall is a small hamlet just outside Staffordshire. Although the bus stop is only used by a handful of people, many are old and frail and shouldn't be exposed to damp conditions whilst waiting for their bus.

The 250 bus is a useful service that travels down the Longton road from Longton to Stone. Officially there is a bus stop just north of Knenhall, which is useful for people like me who live in this village. But in reality there is actually no stop - nor any trace of any stop.
